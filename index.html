Comprehensive reference of 12 major global databases supporting food safety assessment, nutritional analysis, and regulatory compliance. Click on any database title to visit the official website.

USDA FoodData Central

Nutrient

Integrated U.S. food and nutrient data system

Data Foundation Foods, FNDDS, SR Legacy, Branded Foods, Experimental Foods; 117+ nutrient categories
Coverage United States (primary); international branded products
Users Researchers, policymakers, nutrition professionals, product developers
Public Domain CC0 • Free API

WNDDS

Nutrient

Global gateway to national food composition databases

Data Nutrient values from 90+ national databases; proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, minerals
Coverage 92 countries and regions worldwide
Users Researchers, dietitians, students, government officials, database managers
Public Interactive Map

FooDB

Chemical

World's largest public food chemical repository

Data ~24,000 food chemicals; structures, concentration ranges, biological effects, flavor compounds, bioactives
Coverage Global (Canada-based, University of Alberta)
Users Food scientists, metabolomics researchers, nutrition researchers
Public Free w/ Registration

FDA CCT Tool

Chemical

Federal chemical contaminant levels for health risk evaluation

Data Toxic elements, mycotoxins, persistent pesticides, radionuclides; tolerances, action levels, guidance
Coverage United States
Users Food safety professionals, risk assessors, compliance officers
Public Searchable Online

HMDB

Chemical

Comprehensive human metabolome reference including food-derived compounds

Data ~220,000 metabolites; chemical structures, concentrations, biological roles, disease associations
Coverage Global (Canada-based)
Users Metabolomics researchers, clinicians, nutrition scientists, systems biologists
Public Free Download

RASFF

Regulatory

EU-wide rapid information exchange for serious food/feed safety risks

Data Product notifications, hazard types (contaminants, pathogens, allergens), control measures, border rejections
Coverage EU member states + Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Switzerland
Users Food safety authorities, border control, business operators, consumers
Public/Restricted Window since 2020

GSFA Online Database

Regulatory

International food additive standards reference

Data Food additive functional classes, maximum use levels, food category applications, technological justifications
Coverage Global (188 Codex member nations + EU)
Users National regulators, food industry, trade organizations, standard-setters
Public Free Search

ChemLinked

Regulatory

Commercial global food chemical regulations intelligence

Data Food additive regulations, food contact approvals, chemical restrictions, labeling, MRLs
Coverage 100+ countries across Asia-Pacific, Europe, Americas, Middle East, Africa
Users Manufacturers, ingredient suppliers, regulatory affairs, market strategists
Commercial Subscription

EU Pesticides Database

Regulatory

Official EU registry of approved substances and MRLs

Data Active substance approvals, MRLs by commodity, residue definitions, approval expiration dates
Coverage European Union member states
Users Pesticide registrants, food producers, testing laboratories, authorities
Public Searchable Online

OpenFoodTox

Cross-cutting

EFSA open access toxicity database for food chemicals

Data Hazard identification, dose-response data, health-based guidance values, chemical risk assessments
Coverage European Union (global chemical coverage)
Users Risk assessors, researchers, industry toxicologists, regulatory scientists
Public Free Online

FCCmigex

Cross-cutting

Food contact chemical migration evidence repository

Data ~5,000 chemicals, 1,500+ studies; migration data from packaging, concentration ranges, food simulants
Coverage Global (evidence from multiple jurisdictions)
Users Packaging safety researchers, risk assessors, toxicologists, material scientists
Public Free Access

Usage Guidelines

Public Domain (CC0): USDA FoodData Central allows unrestricted use, modification, and distribution without attribution.
Registration Required: FooDB and HMDB require free user registration for full data downloads.
Dual Access: RASFF operates restricted iRASFF for authorities and public RASFF Window for transparency.
Commercial Intelligence: ChemLinked provides tiered subscriptions with varying geographic coverage depth.
